YOUR ONE PAGE GUIDE TO LONDON

Nightlife In London

London Has Some Of The Best Nightlife In The World

Dressing To Impress? Visit Berkeley Square in Mayfair, home of Amazonico, Sexy Fish and Park Chinois

For A Less Dressed Down Evening, check out many of the bars in Shoreditch including Tequila Mockingbird or Barrio Shoreditch

Looking For A Place With A View? Look no further than Madison, Radio Rooftop or Jin Bow Law

Eating Out In London

Celebrating A Special Occasion? Visit COYA, BiBi or KOL

For great streetfood don't forget Spitalfields Market, Borough Market or Mercato Metropolitano

Entertainment In London

London is the home of many great theatres

If you're looking for a laugh visit the (not so secret) Top Secret Comedy Club

The London Apollo, 02 Arena and Wembley Stadium also host a number of fantastic events
